The IARE Advantage

Institute of Aeronautical Engineering (IARE), Hyderabad was established in the year 2000 by a devoted group of eminent professional and industrialists having a long and outstanding experience in educational system with a mission ‘Education for Liberation’. It is the first institute to start B.Tech program in Aeronautical Engineering in the state of Telangana and has gradually transformed itself into a integrated multi-disciplinary technological institute. Founded In 2000, today IARE has a total student strength is 5398 with faculty strength of 345, out of which, 138 are Ph.D (40%) giving rise to healthy faculty student ratio.Ranked in the top 3% of engineering colleges nationally with strong industry connects provide opportunities for every individual to achieve success on their own terms, and prepare them not just for a career, but for life.
